GOT QUESTIONS?

Common Questions

How do I access these programs? +
If your university has an Enbott Center of Excellence, you can enroll directly through your campus coordinator. If not, you can express interest and we'll help facilitate discussions with your administration.
What is CodeChef and how does it help placements? +
CodeChef is a global competitive programming platform with 600,000+ new users yearly. It helps you build problem-solving skills that tech companies value. Our students have secured placements in the 6-12 LPA range by showcasing their CodeChef rankings and certifications.
Are Pearson certificates recognized by employers? +
Yes! Pearson certificates are aligned with Global Scale of English (GSE) and CEFR levels, making them internationally recognized. Employers value these as proof of professional communication ability.
What makes your communication program different? +
Our Pearson program offers 300+ hours across 10 levels with 800+ skill-based tasks. It includes AI-enabled speaking practice with instant feedback, industry-specific scenarios, role-plays, and simulations‚Äînot just grammar lessons.
How does the platform prevent cheating? +
The CodeChef platform has built-in plagiarism prevention with tab control, code similarity checking, and copy-paste blockers, ensuring authentic learning and genuine skill development.
Can I participate in both programs? +
Absolutely! We encourage students to develop both technical problem-solving (CodeChef) and professional communication (Pearson) skills. This combination makes you significantly more competitive.
